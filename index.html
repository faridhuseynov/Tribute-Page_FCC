General Polad Hashimov

Facts about general Polad Hashimov

  • 1975 - Born in Vandam village, Gabala, Azerbaijan
  • 1992 - Graduated from high school, admitted to the Baku Higher Combined Arms Command School
  • 2001 - Received Jubilee medal "10th anniversary of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Azerbaijan"
  • 2002 - Received Medal "For impeccable service" III degree
  • 2003 - Received Medal "For Military Merit"
  • 2007 - Received Medal "For impeccable service" II degree
  • 2008 - Received Jubilee medal "90th anniversary of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Azerbaijan"
  • 2009 - Received Medal "For the Fatherland"
  • 2012 - Received Medal "For impeccable service" I degree
  • 2013 - Received Jubilee medal "95th Anniversary of the Armed Forces of Azerbaijan"
  • 2014 - Received Order "For service to the Fatherland" III degree
  • 2016 - Received Order "For service to the Fatherland" II degree
  • 2017 - Received Medal "Veteran of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Azerbaijan"
  • 2018 - Received Jubilee medal "100th anniversary of the Azerbaijani Army"
  • 2020 - Killed during the fighting on the Armenian-Azerbaijani border in the Tovuz direction
  • 2020 - Awarded with the title National Hero of Azerbaijan
